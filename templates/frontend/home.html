Welcome to KickOutHunger initiative

Inuka is a feeding programme for campus students

Vision

Our vision is to ensure that every university student in Kenya has consistent access to nutritious food, empowering them to excel academically and thrive personally.

Mission

Food insecurity is a significant issue affecting countless university students. Many struggle to afford nutritious meals while balancing the demands of their academic pursuits. We aim to alleviate this burden by connecting these students with donors who are passionate about making a difference in their lives.
